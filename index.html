Financial Capital

Financial Capital

Financial capital most commonly refers to assets needed by a company to provide goods or services, as measured in terms of money value.

Natural Capital

Natural Capital

Natural capital can be defined as the world's stocks of natural assets which include geology, soil, air, water and all living things.

Produced Capital

Produced Capital

Produced capital consists of physical assets generated by applying human productive activities to natural capital and capable of providing a flow of goods or services.

Human Capital

Human Capital

Human capital is an intangible asset or quality not listed on a company's balance sheet.

Social Capital

Social Capital

Social capital is a set of shared values that allows individuals to work together in a group to effectively achieve a common purpose.
